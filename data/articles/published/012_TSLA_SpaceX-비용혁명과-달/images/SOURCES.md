## B_shackleton_crater.jpg
- **Description**: LROC + ShadowCam composite mosaic of Shackleton Crater (lunar south pole). Sunlit rim from LROC, permanently shadowed interior from ShadowCam (200x more light-sensitive). Shows the 21km crater with ~92-95% sunlit rim and -253C interior.
- **Source**: NASA/KARI/Arizona State University
- **NASA page**: https://www.nasa.gov/missions/lro/nasa-moon-camera-mosaic-sheds-light-on-lunar-south-pole/
